## What is the lexicon ecosystem?

<!-- glossary:block id=index-what-is-the-lexicon-ecosystem-paragraph-2 -->
Aster Lang's CNL compiler is **language-agnostic by design**. Every supported language ships as a **lexicon plugin** — a small JSON + Java package defining keyword translations and syntax rules.
<!-- /glossary:block -->

This means you can:

<!-- glossary:block id=index-what-is-the-lexicon-ecosystem-list-item-3 -->
- Translate Aster to your language with ~75 keywords
<!-- /glossary:block -->
<!-- glossary:block id=index-what-is-the-lexicon-ecosystem-list-item-4 -->
- Publish your lexicon as a maven artifact
<!-- /glossary:block -->
<!-- glossary:block id=index-what-is-the-lexicon-ecosystem-list-item-5 -->
- Become an Aster Language Steward
<!-- /glossary:block -->
<!-- glossary:block id=index-what-is-the-lexicon-ecosystem-list-item-6 -->
- Earn ¥3,000 / year platform credit (Steward-exclusive)
<!-- /glossary:block -->
