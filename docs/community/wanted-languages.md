## Don't see your language?

Open a discussion on the [GitHub Discussions board](https://github.com/aster-cloud/aster-lang-core/discussions) — we'll add it to the list and start collecting votes.

## How priorities are set

Priority weight = `community vote count` × 0.4 + `market size` × 0.3 + `existing customer demand` × 0.3.

## Paid lexicon authorship

For languages with sustained community demand but no community author, the Aster team funds **paid lexicon authorship** at ¥8,000 – ¥15,000 + ¥3,000 / year maintenance retainer. Inquire via the same discussion board.
