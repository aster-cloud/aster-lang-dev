## Why public mirrors

We publish the unsigned legal and privacy templates so your legal and security teams can review them **before** the first sales call. A vendor that hides their DPA behind an NDA isn't being thorough — they're being slow.

Customer-specific terms (parties, dates, redlines) are negotiated separately and held in our legal vault, not in the SaaS app.
